Our Rafting Trips

Ready to start your adventure? Contact us now!

Trip 1: Peru Rafting

Embark on a thrilling adventure down the majestic rivers of Peru. Our Peru Rafting trip offers breathtaking views of the Andes Mountains and exhilarating rapids that will challenge even the most experienced rafters. Don't miss the opportunity to explore the vibrant culture and rich history of Peru while experiencing the adrenaline rush of white-water rafting.

Trip 2: Salmon River Experience

Join us for an unforgettable journey along the Salmon River in the heart of Idaho, USA. Our Salmon River Experience combines the thrill of rafting through pristine wilderness with the tranquility of camping under the stars. Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the Sawtooth Mountains and enjoy the serenity of the river as you navigate through thrilling rapids and tranquil stretches of water.

Trip 3: Nile River Explorers

Experience the wonders of ancient Egypt on our Nile River Explorers expedition. Cruise down the legendary Nile River and discover the iconic landmarks of this historic civilization, including the pyramids of Giza and the temples of Luxor. Enjoy a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation as you raft through the Nile's gentle currents and explore the fascinating culture and history of Egypt.
